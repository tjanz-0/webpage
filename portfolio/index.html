Paintings, Designs & Drawings

The digitization of my old paintings and drawings is an ongoing work in progress.

I aim to continously improve this showcase by new additions, better photographs, improved website functionality and so on.

Regarding the artworks: I like to emerge into the styles of different artists, a lot of what is shown are essentially 'case studies'.

For some of the artworks, I didn't come up with some of the showcased motives myself — I try to convey 'what comes from where' in the image descriptions.

In general, I found that I prefer working with some sort of a reference.

I enjoy studying artists, places and objects much more than I do painting 'from nothing'.

Filmmaking

The aim behind this video was to explore fluid-painting from a different perspective.

Contrary to the static works on canvas, video-recording allows to capture the dynamics of the observed flow of colors, not just a snapshot of them in time.

In this way, the abstract spaces forming can be explored with more dimensionality.

Close-up shots and camera movements are used to enhance the immersiveness of these spaces.
