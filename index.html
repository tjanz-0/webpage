about
Hello there, welcome to my website!
I'm Thorge and I'm currently at Heidelberg university as a part time student research assistent (and also to attend some lectures for my MSc).
I've recently finished my BSc in Physics at the Institute of Computer Engineering here in Heidelberg (with the European Institute for Neuromorphic Computing). Here, I was — and still am — working on introducing differentiability to the software simulation and hardware emulation of non-linear neuron dynamics for multi-compartment models.
projects
I also work on some of my own projects in my free time.
